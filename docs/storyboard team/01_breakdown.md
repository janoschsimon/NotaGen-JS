**Tone:** Celebration → Wonder → Terror → Salvation → Peace  
**Characters:** Lilly, Vater, Mutter, Gäste, Kind (O.S.), Fizz

### S03-001

**Movement:** Slow pan across scene

**Location:** Backyard garden with fire pit  
**Props:** Fire pit with flames, string lights, champagne glasses, party decorations, folding chairs  
**Tone:** Festive anticipation, communal celebration  

### S03-002

**Movement:** Rapid tilt up following explosions

**Characters:** Crowd (silhouettes, jubilant)

**Props:** Supernova 3000 fireworks (spectacular explosions), smoke trails

**KEY VISUAL THEMES:**
- Light vs. Darkness (literal and metaphorical)
- Scale contrast (Fizz vs. Supernova, small vs. spectacular)
- Warm red glow as visual motif
- Transformation from commercial warehouse to intimate human connection

**COLOR PALETTE:**
- Scene 01: Muted browns, grays, harsh fluorescents, garish neon accents
- Scene 02: Similar to 01 but warmer when Lilly appears
- Scene 03: Warm party lights → brilliant white/gold (fireworks) → deep black → warm red (Fizz) → gradual return of ambient light

**EMOTIONAL ARC:**
Forgotten → Chosen → Humiliation → Transformation → Salvation → Peace
